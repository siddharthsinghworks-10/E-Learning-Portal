About the E-Learning Management Portal

This portal provides a single place to access course materials, uploaded learning resources, quizzes/assessments, and progress tracking. It supports two roles: Student and Instructor.

How to use (Student)

1) Create your account

Register and select Student as your role.

2) Browse & enroll

Open the Courses page, click View, then press Enroll.

3) Open course content (integrated)

Inside a course, click Open Content to view videos/PDFs inside the portal.

4) Take quizzes

Under Quizzes, click Take Quiz and submit your answers.

5) Track performance

In Dashboard → Quiz Performance, click View Result to see your score and the correct answers.

How to use (Instructor)

1) Create your instructor account

Register and select Instructor as your role.

2) Create a course

Go to Dashboard and click Create New Course.

3) Upload learning materials

Open your course and use Upload Content to add videos, PDFs, or other files.

4) Create & edit quizzes

In your course, click Create Quiz. You can later click Edit Quiz to update questions, choices, and correct answers.

5) Manage and maintain

Use the Manage buttons to quickly open your courses. You can also delete a course from the course page (with confirmation).

Tips

  • Security: Only instructors can create/edit quizzes and upload content.
  • Enrollment: Students must enroll to view course content in the integrated viewer.
  • Results: “View Result” shows correct answers after quiz submission.
